Physics Club, IIT Indore

The Physics Club of IIT Indore is an academic initiative that provides an interactive platform for students to delve into the fascinating world of physics beyond the classroom. Since its establishment on 29th August 2022, the club has aimed to build a vibrant community of physics enthusiasts, fostering exploration, discussion, and experimentation.

Over the years, the club has successfully hosted monthly events funded by the Department of Physics, celebrating a shared passion for the subject.

Vision & Events

Our team is driven by a commitment to innovation and excellence, dedicated to organizing events that inspire and engage. We also commemorate significant occasions like National Science Day, ensuring a mix of educational and celebratory activities.

Leadership and Guidance

The Physics Club has two Faculty Advisors: Dr. Alestin Mawrie and Dr. Onkar Game. These esteemed faculty members provide valuable guidance for major events and initiatives undertaken by the club.

B.Tech Engineering Physics

Overview

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) in Engineering Physics at IIT Indore, introduced in the 2024-25 academic year, is a 4-year program offered by the Department of Physics. This program provides a rigorous education in physics and engineering, equipping students with the expertise to apply advanced physics principles to solve complex engineering challenges.

Program Highlights

  • Research Opportunities: Students gain access to well-equipped laboratories and participate in research projects within the Department of Physics, working on cutting-edge topics alongside faculty.
  • Industry Exposure: The program offers opportunities for internships and industry collaborations, bridging classroom knowledge with real-world applications.

Curriculum

The curriculum is designed to provide a strong foundation in applied physics while incorporating essential engineering concepts. For a detailed syllabus, course outlines, and additional academic information, click here to download the syllabus.

Career Prospects

Graduates of the B.Tech. in Engineering Physics program are well-suited for careers in:

  • Research and Development
  • Semiconductor and Electronics Industry
  • Aerospace and Defense Technology
  • Renewable and Sustainable Energy
  • Advanced Studies and Research in Physics and Engineering

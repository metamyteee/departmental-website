Seminars & Colloquiums

The Department of Physics at IIT Indore regularly organizes colloquia, inviting distinguished scientists, researchers, and industry experts from across the globe. These sessions provide faculty, students, and researchers with opportunities to engage with cutting-edge topics in physics and related fields.

The colloquia serve as a platform for knowledge sharing and discussions on recent advancements, fostering an environment of intellectual growth and collaboration. We encourage everyone in our academic community to attend these events and participate in the exchange of ideas that drive innovation in our department.

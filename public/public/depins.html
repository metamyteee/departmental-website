Department of Physics - IIT Indore

The Department of Physics at IIT Indore is dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation in the physical sciences. With a strong emphasis on interdisciplinary approaches, the department aims to foster a deep understanding of fundamental physics while addressing modern technological challenges.

Academic Programs

The department offers a range of academic programs designed to cater to diverse interests and career goals, including:

  • 4-Year B.S. in Physics: Providing a solid foundation in physics, equipping students for advanced studies or careers in science and technology.
  • B.Tech. in Engineering Physics: Launched in 2024, this program focuses on applying physics principles to engineering problems, with specialization opportunities in cutting-edge areas.
  • Ph.D. Program: Designed for aspiring researchers to pursue innovative studies in theoretical, experimental, and computational physics.

Research and Facilities

The department is a hub for high-impact research in areas such as condensed matter physics, quantum mechanics, astrophysics, and photonics. Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including advanced spectrometers, X-ray diffraction systems, and computational labs, the department supports a thriving research environment.

Colloquia and Outreach

Regular colloquia and workshops bring together leading experts and the academic community to discuss the latest advancements in physics. The department also actively engages in outreach activities, inspiring young minds to explore the wonders of science.
